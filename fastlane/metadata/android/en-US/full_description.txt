<h2>This release needs fresh installation</h2>

<p><b>Payload dumper</b> extracts boot.img and other partition images (boot, vendor_boot, etc.) from a payload.bin or ota.zip directly on your Android device.</p>

- real-time extraction progress
- hash verification for extracted images
- extract directly from OTA zip files
- Debug logging - enhanced troubleshooting and debugging
- Incremental detection - identifies incremental OTAs (extraction not supported yet!)
- Parallel extraction - allow selecting additional images while extraction is in progress
- Cancel extraction - option to abort an ongoing extraction process




<b>Upcoming features:</b>
  - option to select from/to USB storage
